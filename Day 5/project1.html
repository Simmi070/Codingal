Chocolate



  1. Origin
  2. The evolution of chocolate from the past till the present
  3. Common flavours

1 Chocolate's origin traces back over 4,000 years in ancient Mesoamerica (modern Mexico/Central America), where Olmec, Mayan, and Aztec civilizations cultivated cacao beans, grinding them into bitter, spiced drinks for rituals, medicine, and currency.

2After the Olmec and Mayan,Europeans, encountering it in the 16th century, added sugar, transforming the bitter brew into the sweet chocolate we know, with industrialization later creating the solid bars common today.

3Chocolate flavors range from basic types (Milk, Dark, White, Ruby) to complex additions like fruits (Raspberry, Orange, Passion Fruit), nuts (Hazelnut, Almond), sweets (Caramel, Toffee, Nougat, Marshmallow), spices (Mint, Cinnamon), and rich profiles like Coffee, Sea Salt, or even unique ones like Rose or Banoffee Pie, all offering different tastes and textures.
